Our Vision

HealthConnect is revolutionizing healthcare accessibility by bridging the gap between patients and healthcare providers. We envision a future where quality healthcare information and services are accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or economic status.

Through innovative technology and compassionate care, we're building a platform that empowers individuals to take control of their health journey while connecting them with the resources and professionals they need.

About Us

HealthConnect is a student-led startup initiative at Florida International University (FIU), driven by the passion to make healthcare more accessible and equitable. Our team consists of dedicated students from various disciplines who share a common goal: transforming healthcare delivery through technology and innovation.
